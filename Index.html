4 Recommendations

Action Plan

1) Build a Repeatable Interview System

Use a standard structure: role research → skill mapping → STAR stories → mock interviews. Keep everything in one tracker (Notion/Google Sheet).

  • Create a “Top 10 stories” library (impact + metrics).
  • Reuse the same story across multiple questions.
  • Practice a 60–90 second version per story.

2) Master the Top BI Questions

Focus on what shows senior-level strength: metric definitions, governance, stakeholder management, SQL window functions, dashboards, and data quality.

  • Prepare 5 KPI alignment examples (like Marketing vs Sales conflict).
  • Have 3 SQL “showcase” queries ready.
  • Explain your semantic layer approach (Gold model + certified metrics).

3) Improve Offer Rate with Targeted Positioning

Customize your pitch per company: their domain, stack, and business goals. Make your impact measurable and easy to repeat.

  • Use a 30-second “Why me” statement tied to outcomes.
  • Quantify wins (time saved, revenue, retention, risk reduction).
  • Bring a 1-page portfolio summary (Tableau/Power BI + governance).

4) Add Consistency + Confidence with Weekly Practice

Treat interviews like a performance skill: short sessions, frequent repetition, and feedback loops.

  • 3 mock interviews/week (record + review).
  • Daily 20 minutes: SQL + one STAR story.
  • Build an “objections” list and practice responses.
